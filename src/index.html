Our mission

At Dream2Learn, we strive to enable students from all economic backgrounds to succeed in their education. We primarily focus on low-income students. Therefore, we designed our program to be free, effective, and personal to help our students reach their goals and fulfill their academic potential. Learn more by contacting us, and join us in forming a positive change.

Start your learning journey

In order to start your learning process, simply click sign-up through the website. After signing up or contacting us, we will find a volunteer tutor willing to help you learn and succeed. Currently, the tutoring sessions will be online and 1 on 1 using online applications such as zoom or google meets. Our tutors have flexible hours and can personalize their sessions for your accommodations. If you enjoy your sessions, please consider giving us feedback on how we can improve your learning experience.

Courses and programs

For our online program, we offer a variety of courses. We mainly want our students to succeed in English, Social Studies, Math, and Science. In addition, we have tutors that are able to tutor AP courses that might appeal to some students. We are able to tutor our students from 30 minutes up to an hour. We use easy and memorable techniques to help to remember and conceptualize all of the information. If you want to sign up for our free course, please go to the sign-up page.
