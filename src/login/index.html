FAQ

Is the program free?
Yes, the program is entirely free. We are a non-profit organization and we do not charge fees for our services. If you are pleased with our services you can donate to our organization and support us via the donation page.
What can I get helped with?
We have different tutors that can help you in various subjects. If you are struggling in English, Science, Social Studies, or Math; we have the ability to help. Please note that it would be best if you send us your material such as the textbooks, homework, or notes prior to the tutoring session so our tutors can preview the material and give you a better understanding of the topics.
Can I work with the same tutor again?
Absolutely, we schedule multiple sessions with your tutor even if it is your first time to sign up.
